# 🏥 MediNeed

### Offline-First Medical Inventory & Reporting System

> A production-oriented Android application designed to digitize medicine inventory workflows in healthcare facilities — built for environments where reliability matters more than connectivity.

---

## 🚨 Problem

Healthcare facilities—particularly government hospitals and rural clinics—face persistent operational challenges:

* Reliance on **manual registers or fragmented tools (Excel/WhatsApp)**
* **No proactive tracking** of medicine expiry
* **Delayed reporting** to higher authorities
* Inefficient and reactive **reordering processes**
* Limited or unreliable **internet connectivity**

These inefficiencies result in:

* Medicine wastage
* Stock shortages
* Administrative delays
* Increased operational risk

---

## 💡 Solution

**MediNeed** is an **offline-first medical inventory system** that enables healthcare staff to manage medicine stock, track expiry, generate reports, and streamline reordering workflows — all from a single Android device.

It is designed to be:

* **Reliable in low-resource environments**
* **Fast and intuitive for non-technical users**
* **Aligned with real healthcare workflows**

---

## ⚡ Why Offline-First?

Most digital healthcare solutions assume constant connectivity — a flawed assumption in many real-world settings.

MediNeed is built on an **offline-first architecture** to ensure:

* 📡 **Zero dependency on internet availability**
* ⚡ **Instant responsiveness (no network latency)**
* 🔒 **Local data reliability and control**
* 🌍 **Usability in rural and remote healthcare centers**

> Connectivity becomes an enhancement, not a requirement.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 📦 Inventory Management

* Add, update, and manage medicine stock
* Track quantities, batch details, and expiry dates
* Designed for quick, real-time updates in operational settings

---

### ⚠️ Expiry Monitoring

* Identify near-expiry medicines proactively
* Enable timely intervention to reduce wastage and risk

---

### 📊 Report Generation

* Generate structured inventory reports
* Designed for administrative and compliance workflows
* Easily shareable with higher authorities

---

### 🔄 Reordering Workflow

* Detect low-stock medicines
* Assist in preparing structured reorder requests
* Improve supply chain responsiveness

---

### 📱 Lightweight & Practical

* Optimized for real devices used in clinics
* Minimal learning curve for healthcare staff

---

## 📲 Deployment

> MediNeed is distributed as a standalone Android APK for direct installation.

### Quick Start

1. Download the APK from the repository
2. Install on an Android device
3. Launch the application
4. Begin managing inventory immediately

No external setup or internet connectivity required.

## 🧱 System Design

MediNeed follows a clean and maintainable architecture:

* **UI Layer** → Jetpack Compose (declarative, reactive UI)
* **Domain Layer** → Business logic via ViewModels
* **Data Layer** → Room Database for persistent local storage
* **Design Principle** → Offline-first, reliability-focused
